About Cryptomator

Cryptomator provides transparent, client-side encryption for your cloud. Protect your documents from unauthorized access. Cryptomator for iOS is open-source and its code can be reviewed on GitHub. The encryption scheme is publicly documented on Cryptomator's website.

Privacy Policy

Cryptomator does not collect any personal information. In case you use third-party cloud storage services, please refer to the corresponding privacy policy of the provider.
